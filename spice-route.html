About Spice Route Kitchen

Bringing authentic flavors to Charkop since 2010

Our Journey

Established in 2010, Spice Route Kitchen has been Charkop's favorite destination for authentic North Indian cuisine. We started as a small family restaurant with a vision to serve traditional home-cooked meals that remind people of their roots.

Our chefs bring decades of culinary expertise, preparing each dish with carefully selected spices and the freshest ingredients. From rich butter chicken to aromatic biryanis, every meal is a celebration of India's diverse culinary heritage.

We take pride in our tandoor specialties, hand-rolled breads, and signature curries that have become neighborhood favorites. Whether it's a family dinner or a special celebration, we're here to make every meal memorable.
